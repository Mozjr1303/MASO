## Slide 1: Title Slide
- **App Name**: Alchemy Connect
- **Subtitle**: Volunteer & NGO Matching Platform
- **Student Details**: Name, ID, Course (BSC-351)

## Slide 2: Problem & Solution
- **The Problem**: Disconnect between potential volunteers and local organizations needing help. Lack of motivation/tracking for volunteers.
- **The Solution**: A centralized app that makes finding events easy and gamifies the experience ("Alchemy Levels").

## Slide 3: Key Features
- **Role-Based Access**: Separate views for Volunteers and Organizations.
- **Event Management**: Organizations can post, edit, and delete events.
- **Engagement**: Volunteers can join events and track their history.
- **Cross-Platform**: Works on Web and Android.

## Slide 4: Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: React + Vite + Tailwind CSS
- **Backend**: Node.js + Express + SQLite
- **Mobile**: Capacitor (for Android native wrapper)
